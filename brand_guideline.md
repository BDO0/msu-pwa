Apply a consistent and professional brand design to the MSU Aga Khan Museum Interactive QR Code System.

Brand Identity:
The application should reflect a modern, educational, and culturally respectful digital museum experience. The design must be clean, simple, and engaging, suitable for students and visitors.

Color Palette:

Primary Color:

* Maroon (#800000)
* Use for headers, navigation bars, and titles

Secondary Color:

* Green (#2E7D32)
* Use for buttons, clickable elements, and active states

Accent Color:

* Yellow (#FBC02D)
* Use sparingly for highlights such as progress bars, notifications, badges, and important indicators

Neutral Colors:

* White (#FFFFFF) for background
* Light Gray (#F5F5F5) for cards or sections
* Dark Gray (#333333) for text

Color Usage Rules:

* Maroon = structure and hierarchy (headers, top sections)
* Green = actions (buttons, interactive elements)
* Yellow = attention (progress, alerts, completion highlights)
* Avoid overusing yellow to maintain a professional look
* Ensure strong contrast and readability

Typography:

* Use a clean sans-serif font (e.g., Poppins, Roboto, or Open Sans)
* Titles: Bold, large, maroon color
* Subtitles: Medium weight, dark gray
* Body text: Regular, dark gray
* Buttons: Medium weight, white text on green background

UI Style:

* Minimalist and modern
* Mobile-first layout
* Clean spacing and alignment
* Avoid clutter and excessive elements
* Fast-loading and lightweight design

Component Design:

Buttons:

* Green background with white text
* Rounded corners
* Large and easy to tap

Progress Bars:

* Light gray background
* Yellow fill to indicate progress

Viewed Indicators:

* Viewed: Green checkmark or label
* Not viewed: Gray indicator

Cards (for stations and artifacts):

* White or light gray background
* Soft shadows or borders
* Consistent spacing

Completion Badge:

* Use maroon and yellow combination
* Simple and clean design (no heavy graphics)

Layout Guidelines:

Home Page:

* Maroon header
* Welcome message
* Overall progress display
* Station list as cards
* Completers section
* “Play Games” button

Station Page:

* Maroon title
* Artifact list in card format
* Viewed indicators
* Per-station progress display

Artifact Page:

* Large image display
* Artifact title
* Description text
* References section

UX Guidelines:

* Simple and intuitive navigation
* Large buttons for mobile usability
* Clear feedback for user actions
* Fast response and minimal loading delays

Accessibility:

* Ensure readable font sizes
* Maintain high color contrast
* Do not rely on color alone (use icons or labels)

Important Notes:

* Maintain consistency across all pages
* Do not introduce additional colors outside the palette
* Keep the design professional and suitable for a museum environment
* Avoid overly bright, flashy, or distracting visuals
